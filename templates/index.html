About This Tool

Disease Detection

This tool helps farmers identify diseases in cotton and coconut crops by analyzing uploaded field images. It provides:

  • Quick identification of common crop diseases
  • Detailed information about detected diseases
  • Recommendations for appropriate pesticides
Drone Spraying Simulation

The interactive drone simulator allows you to:

  • Visualize your field with highlighted disease hotspots
  • Control a virtual drone to target specific areas
  • Practice precise pesticide application
  • Improve your spraying strategy for real-world applications
